Keep You With Me
2020
Keep You With Me was the first original song released by the group. The song was written by group director Isaac McDougal. The song originally was dedicated to the members of Rifftide as they prepared to end the season, but after the Covid 19 pandemic broke out the group decided to dedicate the song to everyone who wasn't able to say goodbye to someone due to the pandemic.
Original Artist: Rifftide, Written and Arranged by: Isaac McDougal, Mixed by: Becky Willard, Album Art by: Isaac McDougal.

Just Dance / Evacuate the Dancefloor
2019
This song is a mashup of Lady Gaga's "Just Dance" and Cascada's "Evacuate the Dancefloor". The song was originally performed back in the Spring of 2018, but wasn't recorded until 2019.
Original Artists: Lady Gaga & Cascada, Arranged by: Isaac McDougal, Mixed by: Becky Willard, Album Art by: Isaac McDougal.

Dream
2019
Dream was recorded and released just months after it was chosen and arranged. This was Rifftide's first single self-recorded by the group.
Original Artist: Imagine Dragons, Arranged by: Emily Grether Mixed by: Becky Willard, Album Art by: Isaac McDougal.

Move Along
2018
Move Along was the second Rifftide cover selected to be on a Best of BYU A Cappella album. The song was recorded as part of that album's release, and then released as a single a few months later.
Original Artist: The All-American Rejects Arranged by: Isaac McDougal, Mixed by: Becky Willard, Album Art by: Isaac McDougal.

Sing!
2017
Sing! was the first single ever released by Rifftide. It was selected to be on the 2017 Best of BYU A Cappella album. The song was recorded as part of that album's release, and then released as a single a month later. The song remains as Rifftide's most well known cover.
Original Artist: Pentatonix, Arranged by: Scott Shattuck Mixed by: Becky Willard, Album Art by: Isaac McDougal.
